What's This Blog About?

This blog aims to provide updates on all things electronic music. This will be in the form of new tunes in my Spotify playlists, some music events happening and my thoughts on Tracks, EPs, and Albums.

Electronic music covers an enormous amount of genres. The main genres I will cover will range from Techno, Garage, Breaks, Electro, House, and Deep House.

I also update a weekly playlist with 2 friends called Plant Bass'd Picks. We set up this small collective, Plant Bass'd DJs, during Summer 2020 when we started to DJ and share new music together. You can find regularly updated playlists on my Spotify Page. Keep in touch!
