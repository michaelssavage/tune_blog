Tunes of the Week #1

Techno from NARA, HAAi, Luciid and more.

Posted on 16/11/2020

Weekly Updated Spotify Playlist

Each week, I, along with two friends, will add 5 songs each to a spotify playlist. Check out the Plant Bass's Picks Playlist and give it a follow for up to date tunes.
Here, I will focus on my selection of tracks.

NARA - Daddy I Want A Pony

150 BPM Hard Techno.
Buy it on Bandcamp.
Crazy thumping industrial sounding tune ready for any dark venue.

Maddslinky - Compufonique (Part Deux)

125 BPM Tech House.
Buy it on Bandcamp.
Opening with ambient synths, this song works towards a fun chorus with a cool reverbed vocal. Full of evolving plucks and arps.

Luciid - Snitch (Steve Shaden Remix)

138 BPM Driving Techno.
Buy it on Traxsource.
4x4 heavy kicks resembling FJAAK are mingled with a hypnotic siren sound.

hAAi - It's Something We Can All Learn From

128 BPM House.
Buy it on Bandcamp.
Strongly relying on tight percussion and vocal chops, this is a really unique and crazy tune. A North African chant leads the chorus with lots of colour.

W.H. Lung - Inspiration! (Kid Machine Manctalo Rework)

115 BPM Electro House.
Buy it on Bandcamp.
This rework takes all the greatness from the original synth-pop song and packs it full of more life. It turns out being a mish-mash of electronica psychedlic dance. I love it.
