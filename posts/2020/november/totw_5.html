Tunes of the Week #2

A large vocal presence from Kae Tempest and For Those I Love..

Posted on 23/11/2020

Weekly Updated Spotify Playlist

Each week, I, along with two friends, will add 5 songs each to a spotify playlist. Check out the Plant Bass's Picks Playlist and give it a follow for up to date tunes.
Here, I will focus on my selection of tracks.

Wave Corners - Even Mike (Dissolver Remix)

Strong stabby bass and an unintelligible chant give this track a good reason for Nina Kraviz to add it to her Hot Steel mix.
Beatport: 145 BPM Deep Techno.
Buy it on Bandcamp.

Etienne de Crecy - Fast Track

It's hard to imagine this song is 17 years old. The track has an earworm guitar riff played through the whole song with a real Frenchness to the sidechained saw bass.
Beatport: 142 BPM House.
Buy it on Discog.

Meg Ward - Peanut Power

Ready for the club with a light play on the acid and oldskool sounds, this track is packed with energy!
Beatport: 130 BPM House.
Buy it on Traxsource.

For Those I Love - I Have A Love (Overmono Remix)

Hotpress puts its perfectly, "The remixed version adds euphoric, rave-indebted synths to the poetic, vulnerable lyricism that had already marked out the original track as a musical highlight of the year on its release." Overmono are on fire and I can't wait to see what David Balfe (For Those I Love producer) comes up with!
Buy it on iTunes.

High Contrast - Time Is Hardcore (ft. Kae Tempest)

Another poetic and euphoric track but with drum & bass influences. The tune is so good because High Contrast has been making music since 2000 and Kae Tempest brings her ever usual powerful spoken word. The synth might remind you of Jamie XX - Gosh too.
Beatport: 115 BPM Drum & Bass.
Buy it on Beatport.
