Tunes of the Week #4

House elements from Kink, Frits Wentink, and Gerry Read...

Posted on 07/12/2020

Weekly Updated Spotify Playlist

Each week, I, along with two friends, will add 5 songs each to a spotify playlist. Check out the Plant Bass's Picks Playlist and give it a follow for up to date tunes.
Here, I will focus on my selection of tracks.

Octo Octa – River

126 BPM Electronica
A mixing pot full of bloops, bleeps, acid sounds, pads, breakbeats, and lots of love. Octo Octa says "River is about many things. It's about my connection with Eris and a special place we visit often. It's about water and it's ever-changing and shifting nature. It's about my goddaughter, River, who was dancing in the backyard while I played with my x0xb0x for the first time. I saw her youth and excitement and thought about the future and all the healing to come. I wanted to bring all of that movement into the song." Buy it on Bandcamp.

Gerry Read – Sunshine Kissing

126 BPM Jackin House
Real funky! Perfect upbeat tune to get you thinking about Summer 2021. Gerry Read is getting lots of attention in the UK for his solid discography of vibrant, groovy tunes. Sunshine kissing will warm your heart. Buy it on beatport.

Throwing Snow – Murky (Wen Remix)

134 BPM Garage Techno
I love this tune, it's crazy! It has such a dark feeling, as if you’re running away from something dangerous. The siren wailing and evolving kick move this track so well. It takes elements from so many genres and smashes them together. Buy it on Bandcamp.

Jossy Mitsu – whirl

128 BPM leftfield bass
I never really understood what leftfield bass was so I had to look it up. It covers such an array of songs that I could never pinpoint a song and say, “ah yes, leftfield bass”. It’s defined as a high voltage selection of beefy analogue sounds, through a spectrum of weighty drums, heavy hitting basslines, gnarled out synths and some truly twisted FX. Whirl brings a cut up amen breakbeat with rolling bass lines. The song just flows from section to section never letting you take a breath. Buy it on Bandcamp.

Kink – Dreamer (Fritz Wentink remix)

128 BPM House
Never have I ever listened to a track by Kink or Fritz Wentink and disliked it. I knew before listening to this song that I would be adding it to my playlist. It has all the defining characteristics from each artist like that bouncing up and down Wentink bass and Kink's wild repeating synths. It's a fun electro ride! Buy it on Traxsource.
