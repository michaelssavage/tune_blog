Tunes of the Year

My favourite tracks release in 2020..

Posted on 31/12/2020

That was a weird year! At least it meant plenty of time to listen to new tunes and discover new genres. This year, I said my goodbyes to Future House and Bass House and picked up a taste for Drum'n'Bass and more underground stuff like breakbeats and driving techno. I found the time to pick up DJing during the Summer and found a really fun hobby that I could progressively get better at and have a good laugh with some friends. My skills are a work-in-progress but this mix was good fun all the same.

Here are my top 10 releases of 2020, in no particular order.

Nancy Live - Cosmic Beans

135 Driving Techno.
I am ready to go wild when this song is played in a club or festival. It radiates good energy! Buy it on TraxSource.

Dj Streaks - The Best Rapper Alive

126 BPM Deep House.
A delightful Summer tune. It was such a blast playing this song because its such a crowd pleaser. A delicate, wavey tune from Dj Streaks. Buy it on TraxSource.

Cody Currie & Kettama - Harajuku Hammer

128 BPM Groovy House.
A surprisingly groovy piano track from Kettama & Cody Currie. I feel like we get the best of both worlds in this track with Kettama's banging drum sound and Currie's funkiness on top. Buy it on Bandcamp.

For Those I Love - I Have A Love (Overmono Remix)

129 BPM electronica.
This poetic piece gets a great atmospheric remix from Overmono. I can't wait to hear it a festival. Buy it on iTunes.

Special Request - Family Doggo

118 BPM Ambient House.
I loved this ambient track from Special Request. The synths are so soothing throughout the song. Buy it on Bandcamp.

Vladimir Dubyshkin - Lady Of The Night

140 BPM Electro.
This tune will make you move your feet. It's a lot of fun with the weird vocal building up into a WOOH noise in the chorus. Buy it on Bandcamp.

Lxury - Do This Forever

121 BPM Upbeat House.
I enjoyed every release from Lxury this year. His signature bouncing bass sound is so upbeat and groovy. Such a unique artist. Buy it on Bandcamp.

Adelphi Music Factory - The Comedor

130 BPM Energetic House.
Joyous synths in this stomping track. Really liked the whole EP and can't wait for more from this artist. Buy it on Bandcamp.

Mella Dee - Eccy Road

136 BPM House.
Probably my most played track of the Summer. My head was bouncing every time I heard the bass melody play before the incoming kick. Such a fun track. Buy it on Bandcamp.

Pearson Sound - Alien Mode

136 BPM Underground Techno.
The heaviest track in the list, this is prime material for an underground rave. Very spacey and full of attitude. Buy it on Bandcamp.
