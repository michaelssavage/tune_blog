Tunes of the Week #5

117 BPM hitting up to 145 BPM this week ..

Posted on 14/12/2020

Weekly Updated Spotify Playlist

Each week, I, along with two friends, will add 5 songs each to a spotify playlist. Check out the Plant Bass's Picks Playlist and give it a follow for up to date tunes.
Here, I will focus on my selection of tracks.

Jessu Bru – Life’s Alright

2020 Deep House 133 BPM.
Life's Alright is my personal favourite track on the album, Happiness Therapy. The album is a deep house dream from start to finish with dashes of breakbeats and an abundance of piano keys throughout. It does everything right for me as each song glues so well to the next song. Great stuff from Jesse Bru. Buy it on TraxSource.

Genius Of Time – Houston We Have A Problem

2011 House 117 BPM.
Infectious bassline, vocals from Whitney Houston and a conga drum beat. This a really well polished tune that will stand the test of time. Buy it on Clone.nl.

Interplanetary Criminal – Sensational (Breaka Remix)

2019 Garage 130 BPM.
The MC on this UK garage track is vibey! This is a massive remix from Breaka, who is really emerging to be a top name in the rave scene in the past two years. Great use of delay on the open hats and the vocals chops. Buy it on Bandcamp.

Borai – Super Cats Four Five

2017 DnB Breaks 140 BPM.
I can really imagine myself dancing it out to this tune at a rave. It's skanky and messy and absolutely relentless with the break beats. Buy it on Bandcamp.

Dagga – Hype And Dope

2020 Techno 145 BPM.
This monster of a track is LOUD! Dagga shows off on this "neck-breaking gabber" track. Take a listen to the full album, Gutter002, and be blown away by the attack on your ears! Buy it on Bandcamp.
