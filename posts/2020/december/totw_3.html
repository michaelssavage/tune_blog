Tunes of the Week #6

Big Boiler Room Set Influences from Hardsoul and Hapkido..

Posted on 21/12/2020

Weekly Updated Spotify Playlist

Each week, I, along with two friends, will add 5 songs each to a spotify playlist. Check out the Plant Bass's Picks Playlist and give it a follow for up to date tunes.
Here, I will focus on my selection of tracks.

Futers - Mackin'

2019 Ghetto House 128 BPM.
First released in 2016, the album U Get Me balances house, garage and ambient strains of dance. The Mackin’ track is a great ghetto house tune that has reworked the 1995 ‘gangsta funk’ rap track, Shake Junt by Lil Gin. Buy it on Bandcamp.

Blawan - 40 Spiral

2020 Deep Techno 140 BPM.
This tune has a real raw edge to it using what only sounds like a possessed dog barking to me. Dark and hypnotic thumping techno as ever from Blawan. Buy it on Bandcamp.

Hardsoul ft. Ron Carroll - Back Together (Copyright Dub Mix)

2003 Soul House 121 BPM.
A true classic, this House track will make you fall in love with it every time you hear it. I first heard this track when I was watching Peach's Manchester Boiler Room set on Youtube. If you haven't already, drop everything and watch it. You'll hear the soulful vocals and groovy bassline when Peach closes out her amazing set. Buy it on Discogs.

Hapkido - Hurtin' Me

2014 Jackin' House 128 BPM.
I'll never get sick of these hilarious vocals from the 1989 record, Hateful Head Helen. The vocals are beefed up with a swinging groove and classic 4x4 House drums. In Laurent Garnier's Dekmantel Boiler Room set, you can hear him use the acapella so perfectly over the top of another classic House tune, The nightwriters - Let The Music Use You. Buy it on Traxsource.

Letherette - Reflect

2020 Deep House 124 BPM.
Released this year, Mander House Vol. 2 has some seriously good tunes. Reflect is up there as one of my favourites tracks on the album along with Break My Heart and Too Much. Letherette can always be relied on for fun upbeat House music. Buy it on Bandcamp.
