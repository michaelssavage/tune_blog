Iconic Boiler Rooms

The best live dj sets, settings, and crowds.

Posted on 17/11/2020

In no particular order, I share some fun Boiler Room sets. I'd say there has been as much time spent listening to these sets as there has been watching the crowd.

Day 1 of Glitch Festival 2019 was treated to the "Techno Demon" Mall Grab. This set tears up the crowd and brings unrelentless energy. Watch the Boiler Room host, Michail Stangl, remind a dancer about phone etiquette at 1:01:28.

Hard hitting techno duo FJAAK bring a massive set to Poland and show off their signature sound. This guy sums up the mood on the night at 44:16. He seems to be awe struck.

Galway man, Kettama, is a rare talent. His productions are solid and his sets are aggressive and he's only getting better. He's had a sharp rise in popularity in the past 2 years and had the time to show his goofy side on a livestream with Partiboi69 not so long ago. As Kettama closes out Higher Vision festival 2019, this guy tries to conjure a spell to keep the set going for longer at 18:30.

Mezigue bring the vibes and the crowd returns equal amounts back in this set. Mezigue plays six original tunes out of the sixteen played. Watch the crowd go crazy for his original production at 28:40.

Dynamic Duo spread the love, funk and fun in this set. These two represent the trans community, tight mixing and insane track selections. I can't help noticing the group having some nose beers at 12:18.
