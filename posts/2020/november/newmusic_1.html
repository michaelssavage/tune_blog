Finding New Music through Spotify

Using Crabhands for automatic playlist updates and more..

Posted on 21/11/2020

Searching for new music releases is an addiction. How do you keep on top of artist releases when you follow 1500 of them?

A music app like Crabhands comes in very handy. It scans your Spotify account and tracks the artists you follow. As soon as the artist releases a new song, they are added to a special playlist for you!
